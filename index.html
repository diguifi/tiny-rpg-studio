      About Tiny RPG Studio

      A browser-native RPG maker

      Tiny RPG Studio lets you build and instantly play tiny RPGs right in your browser — no installation, no account. Every game is encoded into a single shareable URL.

      Created by André N. Darcie and Diguifi.

      An open-source, free, non-profit engine.
